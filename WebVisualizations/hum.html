Humidity

Perhaps because of the time of year this data was gathered, the humidity of a city trended higher, seemingly regardless of its latitude. However, we can see from the outliers that being farther from the equator (60 deg.) can lend to greater difference -- further below the equator saw high humidity, while higher above the equator saw low humidity.
